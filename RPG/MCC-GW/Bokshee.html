Bokshee

Visible Equiptment

  • Rations for a month
  • Nylon Tent
  • Fire Starting Kit
  • Short Sword
  • Blowgun
  • Plastic Canteen

Description

Bokshee is shy who is more comfortable in the vast terrian of the wilds instead of in towns. His love in life is to travel the world and discover the undiscovered lands and treasures the world offers. He covers the costs of this travels by leading adventureers on trips into the wild for various Archaic Alignments.

Bokshee smokes a corn cob pipe and wears a wide-brimmed hat.
