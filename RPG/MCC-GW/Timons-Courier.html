Visible Equipment

Description

Timon's Courier is a 2.5 meter tall buffalo. They walk on their two hind legs that end in hoofs. Their hands have 4 fingered hands with thumbs. Their heads and shoulders are extremely hairy. Their hands are somewhat less hairy, while their torso and leg are smooth skinned.
