NewCore emerged from stealth with $66 million in funding to address the identity management gap that autonomous AI agents create in enterprise environments. The company argues that as AI agents take on tasks across enterprise systems — approving transactions, routing support tickets, initiating workflows — existing identity and access management (IAM) frameworks, built for human principals, cannot provide adequate credentialing, audit trails, or accountability.

What should I do as a leader? In your next IT or security review, ask specifically how AI agents in production workflows are credentialed and audited. If the answer involves shared service accounts or no per-agent logging, treat that as an open risk item.
