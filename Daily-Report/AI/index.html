VentureBeat published a detailed analysis June 30 of how prompt injection attacks are exploiting the architecture of AI agents, RAG pipelines, and model routing systems. The article argues that enterprises have systematically underestimated the attack surface AI agents create and that traditional security perimeters do not extend to these new systems.

Why it matters: AI agents act on behalf of users and systems, often with access to sensitive data, APIs, and downstream processes.
