A short, grounded explanation of *why this skill should exist*.

## Common Failure Modes

Name the ways people often misuse or misunderstand prompts like this.

* Over-specifying too early
* Treating output as truth instead of input
* Skipping context
* Asking for answers instead of reasoning

This builds trust and credibility.
