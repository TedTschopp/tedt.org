Why this exists

Many explanations of bias and fallacy are accurate but hard to scan. Brain Bugs starts with plain words, short scenes, and low reading load so more people can use the ideas.

What the site tries to do

  • Explain what the bug means.
  • Show what it sounds like.
  • Show where it appears.
  • Offer better questions and calmer responses.

How it scales

The site uses reusable Jekyll layouts, shared includes, and structured front matter so new Brain Bugs can be added without redesigning the site.
