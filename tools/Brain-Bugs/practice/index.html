How Practice Works

Read the short scene. Pick the bug that fits best. Then check the feedback and open the full lesson if you want more detail.

Quizzes

Five to ten scenario cards with instant feedback.

Flashcards

Quick prompts for simple titles, technical terms, and key warning signs.

Spot the Bug

Short quote cards that ask what is going wrong in the reasoning.

Scenario Lab

Longer scenes where several traps may appear at once.
