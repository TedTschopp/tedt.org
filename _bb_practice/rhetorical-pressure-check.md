title: Rhetorical Pressure Check
slug: rhetorical-pressure-check
instructions: Read each scene, choose the best match, and then check the feedback.
questions:
  - prompt: A proposal is criticized, and the reply is, “What about the disaster the other department caused last year?”
    options:
      - Whataboutism
      - Tu Quoque
      - Loaded Language
    answer_index: 0
    explanation: The reply points somewhere else instead of answering the criticism in front of it. That is Whataboutism.
    link: /tools/Brain-Bugs/errors/whataboutism/
  - prompt: A speaker says, “If you really cared about this town, you would support the policy,” but gives little evidence for why it works.
    options:
      - Appeal to Emotion
      - Red Herring
      - Confirmation Bias
    answer_index: 0
    explanation: Caring feelings are being used as the main proof. That is Appeal to Emotion.
    link: /tools/Brain-Bugs/errors/appeal-to-emotion/
  - prompt: A manager answers a complaint by saying, “You made the same mistake last quarter, so you cannot criticize this report.”
    options:
      - Tu Quoque
      - Straw Man
      - Social Proof Bias
    answer_index: 0
    explanation: The criticism is dodged by accusing the critic of hypocrisy. That is Tu Quoque.
    link: /tools/Brain-Bugs/errors/tu-quoque/
  - prompt: A routine cost review gets called “a cruel attack on hardworking people” before anyone explains the actual budget changes.
    options:
      - Loaded Language
      - Hindsight Bias
      - Planning Fallacy
    answer_index: 0
    explanation: Emotion-heavy wording is steering judgment before the facts arrive. That is Loaded Language.
    link: /tools/Brain-Bugs/errors/loaded-language/
  - prompt: A team concern is answered with, “So you want the company to do nothing and collapse?” even though nobody said that.
    options:
      - Straw Man
      - Whataboutism
      - Groupthink
    answer_index: 0
    explanation: The original position was rewritten into an easier target. That is Straw Man.
    link: /tools/Brain-Bugs/errors/straw-man/
