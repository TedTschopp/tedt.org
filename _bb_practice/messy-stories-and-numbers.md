    options:
      - Hindsight Bias
      - Base Rate Neglect
      - Emotional Reasoning
    answer_index: 0
    explanation: The known outcome is rewriting how predictable the event felt at the time. That is Hindsight Bias.
    link: /tools/Brain-Bugs/errors/hindsight-bias/

    explanation: The vivid result is being overweighted while the background odds are ignored. That is Base Rate Neglect.
    link: /tools/Brain-Bugs/errors/base-rate-neglect/

      - Narrative Fallacy

    link: /tools/Brain-Bugs/errors/correlation-vs-causation/
