title: Whataboutism vs Tu Quoque
slug: whataboutism-vs-tu-quoque
left_slug: rhetorical-manipulation
right_slug: rhetorical-manipulation
main_difference: Whataboutism points to some other problem somewhere else, while Tu Quoque points to the critic's own inconsistency.
left_summary: Whataboutism changes the subject by saying, in effect, “What about that other wrong over there?” It widens the frame so the original criticism loses focus.
right_summary: Tu Quoque is narrower. It says, “You do it too,” using hypocrisy as a shield instead of answering the criticism itself.
confusion_example: A manager is asked about missed goals and replies, “What about the sales team missing theirs last quarter?” That is Whataboutism. If the manager says, “You missed goals before too, so you cannot criticize me,” that is Tu Quoque.
memory_trick: Whataboutism points away. Tu Quoque points back.
