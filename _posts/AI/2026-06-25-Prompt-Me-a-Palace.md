*Prompt Me a Palace* is a dazzling hip-hop musical-theater creation number that begins playful, fast, and seductive, driven by crisp percussion, bright piano, synth pulses, and sparkling digital textures. The Vibe Engineer leads with quick, clever rap verses that feel like live invention: every prompt becomes a prototype, every idea turns into motion.

The AI Ensemble sings and chants in layered, machine-like patterns, repeating phrases with hypnotic precision. Early choruses feel triumphant and almost magical, with big theatrical lift and rapid call-and-response. The point is not that the machine is evil. The point is that speed feels like magic until the thing being built starts building beyond the map.

As the song progresses, the music darkens: bass deepens, rhythms tighten, harmonies become uneasy, and the repeated AI phrases start to feel overwhelming. The final section shifts from solo brilliance to collaborative resolve, showing that AI speed needs architecture, security, data, operations, and IT to become something that lasts.
