*Build It. Ship It.* is a high-energy opening number with a hip-hop musical-theater style: fast ensemble rap, punchy sung hooks, overlapping character lines, and a driving beat that feels like a company waking up at full speed. The music should start with whispered rhythmic fragments, then build into layered percussion, piano stabs, orchestral hits, bass, and digital textures.

Each group has its own vocal flavor. Citizen Builders are urgent and practical. AI Builders are quick and dazzling. IT is precise and grounded. Security is sharp. Operations is low and steady. Data is crisp. Architecture is measured. The chorus should feel big, clever, and communal, like a whole enterprise moving in rhythm.

By the end, the energy turns from excitement to tension, landing on the question: if everyone can build, who keeps it together?
